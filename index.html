Visit Nepal

Pokhara

Pokhara, the "City of Lakes," is a paradise with serene lakes, breathtaking mountain views, and adventure sports. The reflection of Mt. Machhapuchhre on Phewa Lake, the peace of World Peace Pagoda, and the thrill of paragliding make it a dream destination.

Ghalegaun

Ghalegaun, a picturesque Gurung village, offers a raw Himalayan experience with stunning views of Annapurna and Manaslu ranges. Traditional houses, warm hospitality, and cultural richness make it a perfect retreat for nature lovers and cultural explorers
